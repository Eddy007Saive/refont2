Annotation of Medical Data

Annotations play a key role in training artificial intelligence models used for analyzing medical data, whether for disease detection, research, or improving healthcare.

Learn More

Medical Image Annotation

We use image annotations to add contextual or descriptive information to an image. They are commonly used in fields such as object recognition, image analysis, and more.

Medical Video Annotation

We add video annotations in the form of metadata to video sequences, enabling the description of actions, objects, or events within the video. This process is essential for training AI models specialized in computer vision.

Medical Audio Annotation

We use audio annotations to transcribe medical consultations, identify symptoms from body sounds, or transcribe exchanges between doctors and patients in a clinical context.

3D Annotation in Healthcare

3D annotations are used to create 3D anatomical models, which can be used for planning complex surgical operations, training doctors, and simulating procedures before performing them in reality.
