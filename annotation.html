Data Annotation

At our company, data annotation involves adding labels, descriptions, or additional information to raw data (images, videos, audio, etc.) to make it understandable for AI models. We understand that these annotations are crucial for training artificial intelligence models, as they allow supervised learning algorithms to comprehend and predict outcomes from new data.

About Image Annotation

We use image annotations to add contextual or descriptive information to an image. They are commonly used in fields such as object recognition, image analysis, and more.

About Video Annotation

We add video annotations in the form of metadata to video sequences, enabling the description of actions, objects, or events within the video. This process is essential for training AI models specialized in computer vision.

About Audio Annotation

We enhance audio recordings by adding descriptions or labels, such as transcriptions or sound classifications. These annotations play a key role in developing AI models capable of analyzing complex audio signals.

About 3D Annotation

We use 3D annotations to provide detailed information about objects or scenes in three dimensions. They play a fundamental role in fields such as virtual reality, 3D modeling, and simulations.
